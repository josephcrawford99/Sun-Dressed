Sun Dressed Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2025

Quick Summary: Sun Dressed helps you choose weather-appropriate outfits using AI. We collect your location for weather data, store your preferences locally, and use AI services to generate outfit recommendations. We don't sell your data or share it unnecessarily.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Information

We collect your location data to provide accurate weather information for outfit recommendations. This includes:

  • Precise Location: GPS coordinates when you allow location access
  • Approximate Location: City/region when you search for locations manually
  • Weather Zone Data: Temperature, humidity, and weather conditions for your area

1.2 User Preferences

We store your preferences locally on your device to improve recommendations:

  • Temperature units (Celsius/Fahrenheit)
  • Style preferences and feedback on outfit suggestions
  • Trip information and packing lists you create
  • Clothing items you mark as "don't have" or "don't like"

1.3 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name and email address (via Apple Sign In or email registration)
  • Authentication tokens for secure access
  • Account preferences and settings

1.4 Usage Data

We automatically collect limited usage information:

  • App performance data and crash reports
  • Feature usage statistics (anonymized)
  • API request logs for troubleshooting

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Weather-Based Recommendations: Your location provides weather data for AI outfit generation
  • Personalization: Your preferences help tailor outfit suggestions to your style
  • Trip Planning: Store and organize your travel plans and packing lists

2.2 AI-Powered Features

We use Google's Gemini AI service to generate outfit recommendations. This involves:

  • Sending weather data and your style preferences to Google's servers
  • Processing your requests to generate appropriate clothing suggestions
  • All AI processing follows our safety guidelines to avoid inappropriate content
AI Content Notice: Outfit recommendations are generated by artificial intelligence and may not always be perfect. Use your judgment when following suggestions.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Third-Party Services

We share limited data with these trusted partners to provide our services:

  • OpenWeather API: Location coordinates for weather data
  • Google Places API: Location queries for autocomplete suggestions
  • Google Gemini AI: Weather conditions and preferences for outfit generation
  • Apple Sign In: Authentication tokens (Apple handles your credentials)

3.2 Data We Don't Share

We will never:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your location with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes beyond our own app
  • Access your photos, contacts, or other unrelated device data

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, such as responding to subpoenas or protecting against fraud.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Most of your data stays on your device:

  • Preferences and settings stored locally using AsyncStorage
  • Trip information and packing lists stored locally
  • Outfit recommendations cached locally to reduce API costs

4.2 Cloud Storage

Minimal data is stored in the cloud:

  • Account information (name, email) stored securely with Supabase
  • Authentication tokens for secure login
  • Sync data for cross-device access (when implemented)

4.3 Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication tokens are securely stored
  • Regular security audits of our code and infrastructure
  • Limited data retention policies

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access and Control

You can:

  • View and edit your account information in app settings
  • Delete your trips and preferences at any time
  • Disable location access (app will still work with manual location entry)
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us

5.2 Data Deletion

You can delete your data by:

  • Deleting the app (removes all local data)
  • Requesting account deletion via email
  • We will delete server-side data within 30 days of request

5.3 EU/GDPR Rights

If you're in the EU, you have additional rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of incorrect data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

6. Children's Privacy

Sun Dressed is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate. These transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Adequacy decisions
  • Certification schemes

8. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Usage Data: Anonymized after 90 days
  • Crash Reports: Deleted after 30 days
  • API Logs: Deleted after 7 days

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for significant changes
  • Posting updates on our website

10. Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data:

  • Email: sundressed.app@gmail.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

11. Third-Party Privacy Policies

Our app integrates with third-party services. Please review their privacy policies:

  • OpenWeather Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy (for Gemini AI and Places API)
  • Apple Privacy Policy (for Sign In with Apple)
  • Supabase Privacy Policy

This privacy policy was last updated on July 7, 2025, and is compliant with Apple App Store requirements, GDPR, and California privacy laws.
