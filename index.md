# Privacy Policy for Sun Dressed

**Last Updated:** November 6, 2025

## Introduction

Sun Dressed ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **What we collect:** Your device's geographic location (latitude and longitude)
- **How we collect it:** Through your device's location services when you grant permission
- **Why we collect it:** To fetch weather data for your area and provide accurate outfit recommendations
- **How it's used:** Location data is sent to OpenWeatherMap API to retrieve current weather conditions
- **Storage:** Location data is not stored permanently; it's only used for the current session

### User Preferences
- **What we collect:**
  - Style preference (masculine, feminine, or neutral)
  - Temperature format preference (Celsius or Fahrenheit)
  - Optional activity information (what you're planning to do)
- **Storage:** Stored locally on your device using secure local storage
- **Sharing:** Never shared with third parties

## Third-Party Services

### OpenWeatherMap API
- **Purpose:** Provides weather data based on your location
- **Data shared:** Your geographic coordinates
- **Privacy Policy:** https://openweathermap.org/privacy-policy

### Google Gemini API
- **Purpose:** Generates personalized outfit recommendations based on weather and your preferences
- **Data shared:** Weather information, your style preferences, and optional activity description
- **Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Storage and Security

- All user preferences are stored locally on your device
- We do not maintain servers or databases that store your personal information
- Location data is transmitted securely (HTTPS) to weather API providers
- No personal identifying information is collected or stored

## Data Sharing

We do not:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with advertisers
- Store your location history
- Track your usage across other apps or websites
- Require account creation or authentication

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Deny location access:** The app will not function without location permission, as weather data is essential for outfit recommendations
- **Clear your data:** Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored preferences
- **Change preferences:** Update your style and temperature preferences at any time within the app

## Children's Privacy

Sun Dressed does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app is intended for general audiences.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
